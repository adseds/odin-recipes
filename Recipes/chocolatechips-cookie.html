Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are the litimate comfort dessert. With their crisp edges, soft centers, and gooey chocolate chunks, they’re a timeless treat for all ages. Whether served with a glass of cold milk or warm straight from the oven, these cookies are irresistible.

ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup of granliated sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, granliated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the flour, baking soda, and salt to the wet ingredients. Mix until combined.
  4. Gently fold the chocolate chips into the dough until evenly distributed.
  5. Drop spoonflis of cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 8–10 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  6. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack. Serve warm or store in an airtight container.
